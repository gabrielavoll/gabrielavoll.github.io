About Me

I am a native New Yorker, who studied Computer Science and Studio Art at Hunter College. I am a big indie comics fan, expert papier-mâchér of masks and a semi-fluent Spanish speaker, to the dismay of most of my family. Who knew Spanish was so much harder then C++.

I am working at a great company, Book of the Month.

As a side project, I'm working on a Unity video game, that I have had jangling around in the back of my head for a while. It would be set in Dante's Inferno, and besides that the particulars are still stewing. I also maintain a web-app I made in the summer of 2014, which shows you events that are happening in the area ( mostly free and mostly art-related) on any given day, in the New York City Area.
